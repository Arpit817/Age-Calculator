# 🌟 Age Calculator

A modern, visually stunning, and highly accurate Age Calculator built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. 

## ✨ Features

- **Detailed Age Calculation:** Calculates exact age in **Years, Months, and Days**.
- **Next Birthday Countdown:** Automatically computes how many months and days are left until your next birthday.
- **Zodiac Sign Identifier:** Determines your astrological Zodiac sign based on your date of birth.
- **Glassmorphism UI:** A sleek, frosted-glass effect with a dynamic, animated gradient background.
- **Input Validation:** Prevents the selection of future dates and handles leap years perfectly.

## 🚀 Technologies Used

- **HTML5:** Semantic structure and layout.
- **CSS3:** Advanced styling, Glassmorphism, flexbox, and keyframe animations.
- **JavaScript (ES6):** Core logic for date manipulation, DOM updates, and custom animations.
- **Font Awesome:** For elegant icons.
- **Google Fonts (Poppins):** For premium and highly readable typography.
